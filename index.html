Features that care for you.

Designed to be simple and reliable, because in an emergency, you shouldn't have to think twice.

One-Tap Emergency Alert

If you feel unsafe, simply press the SOS button. Your exact location, battery level, and an audio snippet are instantly sent to your trusted family members and local authorities.

Verified Safe Routes

Instead of the fastest route, we guide you through well-lit, busy streets verified by other women in the community.

Private by Design

Your location is yours. We don't track you in the background or sell your data. Location is shared only when YOU choose to.

Your Trusted Circle

Add your parents, siblings, or close friends to your 'Trusted Circle'. They will get notified when you start a late-night commute and when you reach home safely.
